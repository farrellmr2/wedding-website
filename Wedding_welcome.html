Mackenzie and David

We're getting married!

We met at a Crossfit gym in Richmond, VA in 2008, but didn't start dating until nearly 2 years later. Mackenzie was an art school hipster and David was a PhD student; not exactly a match made in heaven. But after a few months of pursuit (by Mackenzie) we officially became a couple in June of 2010.

We share many things in common and we really love the beach (crossing our fingers for some wedding waves), which is why we're getting married in beautiful Charleston, SC!
